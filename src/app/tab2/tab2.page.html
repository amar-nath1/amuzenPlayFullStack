Tab 2 Program Rules & Benefits Program Rules How the game + investment works

1. Purchase

User pays Rs.15 to obtain the locked card number.

2. Payment Window

Payments are allowed only from the 1st to the 10th of each month. After the 10th the Pay button is disabled.

3. Winners Announcement

Winners are announced on the 15th of each month at 9:00 AM.

4. Amount Breakdown

From Rs.15: Rs.9 is invested (user can withdraw later), Rs.1 covers expenses, Rs.5 is non-refundable and funds the prize pool.

5. Winners Selection

10% of total participants are chosen as winners each month.

6. Prize Types

Prizes vary in size and type (cash, gifts, other valuables) as decided by the admin team.

 Benefits of the Program Why join the program

Power of Unity

Small monthly investments (Rs.9 each) pooled together can create substantial capital invested across diversified instruments (mutual funds, ETFs, bonds, gold, etc.), growing significantly over time.

Community Strength

Be part of a strong, like-minded group with shared financial goals.

Events & Meetups

Periodic events, meetups and knowledge-sharing sessions to strengthen the community.

Long-term Growth

A disciplined monthly collective investment approach can produce compounding returns and meaningful wealth creation for participants.
